AI Lab

The AI Lab at SDU University is a hub for innovation, research, and development in the field of Artificial Intelligence. It provides students with the opportunity to explore AI technologies, work on real-world projects, and collaborate with like-minded individuals.Our lab focuses on machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and AI-driven solutions. We host workshops, hackathons, and research initiatives to help students enhance their skills and gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced AI enthusiast, the AI Lab is the perfect place to expand your knowledge, build projects, and contribute to the future of artificial intelligence!
