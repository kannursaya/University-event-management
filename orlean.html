Orlean

The Orlean Dance Club is a special space at SDU University, created exclusively for girls who love dance and self-expression through movement. This club is a supportive and empowering community where members can explore various dance styles, from contemporary and jazz to hip-hop and cultural dances. We offer regular dance workshops, choreography sessions, and performance opportunities, allowing our members to grow in confidence, skill, and creativity. Whether you're an experienced dancer or just starting your journey, the Orlean Dance Club is the perfect place to move, connect, and shine!
