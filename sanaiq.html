SanaIQ

SanaIQ Club is the perfect place for students who love intellectual challenges, strategy games, and brain-teasing puzzles. Our club focuses on developing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and logical reasoning through IQ tests, chess, riddles, escape room challenges, and strategic games.We believe that intelligence is not just about knowledge but also about adaptability and creativity. Through competitions, group challenges, and fun interactive sessions, members enhance their cognitive abilities while enjoying a competitive and friendly environment. If you love a good mental challenge, SanaIQ Club is the place to be!
